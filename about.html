Origin

I registered the domain name pnuo.com in 2006 when the concept of Web2 was prevailing. I planned to start a business to provide tourism services, but things didn't go as planned, so the domain was only used for personal use for a long time.

Now, in the era of Web3, I'm still as excited as I was a decade ago about the progress and evolution of the Internet, so I made this simple website without any particular goal, as an entrance for note-taking and service recommendation for everyone.
